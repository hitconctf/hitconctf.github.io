  • Date: Dec 5 and Dec 6, 2015
  • Venue: Taipei. Nangang Exhibition Center, 5th Floor.
    •            ( Taipei MRT Nangang Exhibition Center Station )
               No.1, Jingmao 2nd Rd., Nangang District,

  • Type: Attack & Defense ( 4 Players / Team )

The top ten winning teams from the qualification round are invited to participate in the on-site CTF Finals. The Attack and Defense format competition will see the world’s best teams competing for the 2015 HITCON CTF Championship.

Moreover, the Champion team will be entitled for the 2016 DEF CON CTF and SEC CON CTF 2015 Finals.

This is a match of the masters, definitely not to be missed!
